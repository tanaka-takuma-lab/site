単位球面上で相互作用する粒子のデモ　Demonstration of collective motion of particles interacting on a unit sphere

Numerical simulation of the system of New Journal of Physics 16, 023016 (2014) [Link] [PDF] with N=20 particles on a 3D sphere. Reloading the page initializes the simulation with random parameters and initial values.
